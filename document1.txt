# Smart Home Hub X1000 - Product Specifications

The Smart Home Hub X1000 is our flagship home automation controller designed to integrate all your smart devices into one seamless ecosystem.

## Technical Specifications
- Processor: Quad-core ARM Cortex-A53, 1.4GHz
- Memory: 2GB RAM
- Storage: 16GB eMMC Flash
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax), Bluetooth 5.2, Zigbee 3.0, Z-Wave Plus, Thread
- Ports: 2x USB 3.0, Ethernet (Gigabit), HDMI 2.0
- Power: 5V DC, 2.5A
- Dimensions: 4.5" x 4.5" x 1.0" (114mm x 114mm x 25mm)
- Weight: 210g

## Key Features
- Universal compatibility with over 10,000 smart devices
- Voice control integration with all major assistants (Alexa, Google Assistant, Siri)
- Advanced automation routines with conditional logic
- Energy monitoring and optimization
- Secure local processing with end-to-end encryption
- Remote access via dedicated mobile app (iOS and Android)
- Weekly automatic security updates
- Custom scene creation with timing and sensor triggers

## System Requirements
- High-speed internet connection (minimum 10 Mbps)
- 2.4GHz and 5GHz Wi-Fi network
- Mobile device running iOS 14+ or Android 10+

The Smart Home Hub X1000 comes with a standard 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranty options are available through our Premium Care program.
